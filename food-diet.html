Pet Food & Diet

🐶 Dog Diet Plan

  • Morning: Dry kibble with water
  • Afternoon: Cooked chicken and rice
  • Evening: Wet dog food and veggies

🐱 Cat Diet Plan

  • Morning: Wet canned food with tuna
  • Afternoon: Dry kibble & fresh water
  • Evening: Cooked fish & small veggies

💡 Tips:

  • Always provide fresh water
  • Avoid chocolates, onions, and grapes
  • Use age-specific pet food (puppy/kitten/adult)
